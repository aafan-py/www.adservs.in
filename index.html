Service Implicit

It is used to send informational SMS by an Individual/Brand to their registered customers, OTP's, Order alerts, Booking confirmation and other related messages are now sent via Implicit category or SMS type. In this SMS are delivered to all recipients, irrespective of their DND status with brand Sender ID. SMS are delivered 24x7 with no time restriction.

Service Explicit

Send your promotional or offer related messages with brand Sender ID using Explicit service type or SMS type, You can upload your concent based contact numbers on any of DLT portal. Register template using consent. Operators charge additional amount depending on quantity, You can refer rate chart on their official websites. *T&C Apply.

Transactional

Since DLT(Distributed Ledger Technology) is in force, Transactional route is now a dedicated route assigned only to Banks. Only banks can use Transactional route to send OTP's, alerts & other banking related messages to their customers. Adding Header ID using Transactional Route is only available for Bank. Other's can use Implicit SMS services for the same.

Read More

SIM Based SMS

Get bulk SIM Based SMS solution for Promotion & Marketing SMS and send bulk SIM Based text messages to your Non-DND customers on the go with 10 digits implicit Indian mobile numbers, Sending time is 8AM to 8PM. No DLT registration required, No Sender ID or Content Template approval required, Only you just need to do KYC with minimal documents.
